Testimonials

These testimonials reflect professional experiences and collaborations with national and international institutions, highlighting leadership, academic excellence, and contributions to sustainable development, education, and research.

Professor Ginigaddara provided outstanding leadership as the Team Leader in developing nationally significant Career Guidance and Counseling academic programs in collaboration with the University of Colombo. Her academic vision, curriculum expertise, and commitment to learner-centered education laid a strong foundation for certificate, diploma, and degree pathways that address Sri Lanka's evolving education and workforce needs. Her professionalism, collaborative approach, and dedication have made a lasting impact on the project and the broader education and human resource development landscape.

Nirosha Hapuarachchi, Director - Career Guidance, YouLead (USAID Project)

Professor Ginigaddara demonstrated exceptional leadership and guidance in the successful completion of the Healthy Landscapes Project, which focused on managing agricultural landscapes in socio-ecologically sensitive areas. Her ability to effectively coordinate multidisciplinary teams, integrate research outcomes into national academic curricula, and ensure strong collaboration among government, academic, and international stakeholders significantly strengthened the project's impact. Her dedication, strategic vision, and commitment to sustainable land and water management were instrumental in achieving the project's objectives and advancing national capacity in environmental conservation.

A. J. Silva, Project Manager - Healthy Landscapes Project

We sincerely appreciate the valuable contribution rendered as a resource person for the Agricultural Science Teacher Training Program conducted by the Provincial Department of Education, Kurunegala. The session on Sustainable Agriculture was highly informative and engaging, significantly enhancing the professional knowledge and teaching practices of G.C.E. (A/L) Agricultural Science teachers across the North Western Province. The expertise, commitment, and collaborative support provided were instrumental to the success of the program, and we look forward to continued engagement in future professional development initiatives.

Dr. A. M. D. Wedanda, Additional Provincial Director of Education (Development)
